14:T985,<h2>前言</h2>
<p>买了个轻量应用服务器，想着搞点东西玩玩</p>
<h2>下载</h2>
<ol>
<li>服务端压缩包
https://www.teamspeak.com/en/downloads/#server</li>
<li>客户端
https://www.teamspeak.com/en/downloads/#ts3client</li>
</ol>
<h2>安装部署</h2>
<ol>
<li>上传服务端压缩包到Linux服务器，解压</li>
</ol>
<pre><code class="language-bash">tar xvf teamspeak3-server_linux_amd64-3.13.7.tar.bz2
</code></pre>
<ol start="2">
<li>不建议使用root启动服务，创建一个teamspeak用户</li>
</ol>
<pre><code class="language-bash">mv teamspeak3-server_linux_amd64 /home/teamspeak/teamspeak3
useradd teamspeak
passwd teamspeak
chown -R teamspeak:teamspeak /home/teamspeak/teamspeak3/
su teamspeak
</code></pre>
<ol start="3">
<li>创建同意许可条款文件</li>
</ol>
<pre><code class="language-bash">cd /home/teamspeak/teamspeak3
touch .ts3server_license_accepted
</code></pre>
<ol start="4">
<li>启动服务</li>
</ol>
<pre><code class="language-bash">./ts3server_startscript.sh start
</code></pre>
<ol start="5">
<li>第一次启动后会有秘钥和管理员账号密码，需要将其记下，后面用到</li>
</ol>
<pre><code class="language-bash">----------------------------------------------------------------- 
I M P O R T A N T 
------------------------------------------------------------------ 
Server Query Admin Account created 
loginname= "666", password= "666" 
apikey= "666" 
------------------------------------------------------------------ 
------------------------------------------------------------------ 
I M P O R T A N T 
------------------------------------------------------------------ 
ServerAdmin privilege key created, please use it to gain 
serveradmin rights for your virtualserver. please 
also check the doc/privilegekey_guide.txt for details. 
token=666
------------------------------------------------------------------ 
</code></pre>
<ol start="7">
<li>服务器需要打开UDP 9987端口</li>
</ol>
<h2>客户端连接</h2>
<ol>
<li>打开TS客户端，点击连接菜单，输入服务器IP或者域名，Nickname可以自定义,首次连接不需要输入密码
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/qingnichimi/pictrue/master/blog/1706855898308.jpg" alt=""></li>
<li>连接成功后会要求输入token，即上述启动成功后所显示的</li>
<li>这样就完成TS的服务搭建。其他的具体用法还需要慢慢研究。</li>
</ol>
